Database Management Course

Learn the fundamentals of database management, covering various types of databases, their architectures, and how databases are essential for modern business applications.

What You'll Learn

  • Understand the basic concepts of databases and their applications.
  • Learn SQL for creating, managing, and manipulating relational databases.
  • Explore NoSQL databases and understand when to use them over traditional relational databases.
  • Study database security best practices to protect data integrity and privacy.
  • Implement a real-world project using a modern database system.
